About Us

How we came to be

Welcome to VhoTel, a legendary hotel in Jakarta that has been open since 1999. We take immense pride in providing impeccable service and creating unforgettable experiences for our esteemed guests. Our story began with a vision to redefine luxury hospitality in Jakarta. Years of meticulous planning, attention to detail, and unwavering commitment culminated in the grand opening of VhoTel in 1999. Since then, we continue to try to be more than just a hotel; we aspire to become a sanctuary where every guest feels at home, pampered, and valued.

Our motto

At VhoTel, our motto is to elevate hospitality to extraordinary heights. We firmly believe that every guest deserves an exceptional experience that goes beyond expectations. With a relentless commitment to service excellence, we strive to create an environment where luxury meets warmth, and every detail is meticulously crafted to delight our guests. From the moment you step into our hotel, you will be embraced by our dedicated team, who are driven by a passion for providing personalized care and creating unforgettable memories. Welcome to VhoTel, where we redefine hospitality and transform your visit into an extraordinary journey.

Our founding father

Ryan, the visionary behind VhoTel, is our esteemed founding father who embarked on a journey to create a remarkable hospitality experience. With a profound passion for service and a keen eye for detail, Ryan envisioned a haven of luxury and comfort in the heart of Jakarta. He meticulously curated every aspect of VhoTel, from its exquisite design to its unparalleled amenities, ensuring that every guest would feel embraced by a sense of warmth and elegance. At VhoTel, we honor Ryan's vision by providing impeccable service and creating unforgettable moments for each and every guest who walks through our doors.
